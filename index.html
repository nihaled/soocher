Frequently Asked Question (FAQ)

Soocher is a telemedicine app that lets you consult trusted doctors from Kerala for medical advice and second opinions. Consultations can be done through video call, chat, or voice message, and you can easily share your reports and scans with doctors as well. With licensed specialists across multiple fields, Soocher makes healthcare from Kerala accessible anytime, anywhere.

You can easily consult with doctors through our app:
- Browse and select your preferred doctor or specialty.
- Book your slot for a video consultation.
- During the session, you can talk via video call, chat, or voice message.
- You can also upload and share medical reports, scans, or lab results directly in the app for the doctor to review.

- Our secure payment gateway accepts most International and Local bank cards.
- UPI payments are also supported.
- In case of any difficulty while paying or confirming your booking, our support team will assist you right away through the in-app help or WhatsApp support.

Booking a consultation is simple:
- Select your specialty (like General Medicine, Pediatrics, Gynecology, etc.) or choose a specific doctor.
- Pick an available time slot that suits you.
- Complete the payment using any bank card or UPI.
- Your appointment will be instantly confirmed in the app.
